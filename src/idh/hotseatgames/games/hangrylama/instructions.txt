The player is confronted with a hangry lama. It wants something to eat!
Give the lama some food or it will bite you. This spceial kind of lama
eats letters! Each round the player has 3 seconds to input the letters 
the lama wants. There are up to 8 rounds for each player. 
In the first round the lama asks for one letter, then for two and so on.
Complete all 8 rounds to make the lama happy.
Each completed round gives one point.
