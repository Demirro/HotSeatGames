The player plays Black Jack against the dealer. The player goes first. 
They get 2 open cards and have to decide whether they draw or not. 
The goal is to get as close as possible to a total of 21 points, 
but if they get more than 21 points, they instantly lose. 
Ace is 11 points, King, Queen and Jack are 10 points. 
The other cards' values are as indicated on the card. 
A win is worth 5 points, a draw is worth 3 points!
